INFLATION

Inflation is the decline of purchasing power of a given currency over time.

Inflation occurs when there is a rise in the price of most goods and services of daily or common use, such as food, clothing, housing, recreation, transport, consumer staples, etc, hence reducing the purchase value of the currency.
